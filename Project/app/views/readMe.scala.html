Welcome to FREEZER - your dating app on cold and enjoyful winterdays in the mountains.

I may sound like an enemy in Dragon Ball Z, but I'm much more!

Come with me and discover my functionalities.

I. How to begin?

  • First you have to accomplish the registration.
  • After registration you have to login.
  • You will be redirected to the home overview after successfully logging in.
  • And don't worry: We encrypt your password and personal data on a very high security level.

II. On the home overview

Your location should be found automatically and based upon your location all available lifts near you will be displayed in a select menu - if not, click refresh.

After choosing the lift you want to ride with, click OK, to get all users near the chosen lift which also use Freezer.

Now you can set a filter to match all found users to your wanted criterias and interests. You can also click on the username of the found users wo get information about them (Name, Age, prefered interests, ...).

III. How to contact

If you find users who match with your interests, you can easily contact them by our contact request functionality. You can write them messages with the proposed time and lift and you can also receive messages.

If you got new messages, the counter on the top (near the bird) will change and directly lead you to your message list. There you can find all incoming messages in a nice list.

You also can open them from there and view them in detail and you also can directly reply to the user's message.

IV. Update your settings

You also can change your personal settings on account, personal data (profile) and the matching options easily. Feel free to navigate through the different setting pages by an improved menu which can be used by touch.

V. Conclusion

You may ask "This is it?" - Yes this is it for now. We gave our best to give you a cool and "freezing" tool to come in contact with other people during your winter activities.

We hope you will enjoy it and of course, we are working hard for further improvements.

If you have some interesting ideas for improvement or if you find a bug, please don't hesitate and contact us via email.

Last, but not least: This app of course is free and does not cost you any money!

And now - have fun and ...
